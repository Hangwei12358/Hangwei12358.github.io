Hi there, my name is Hangwei QIAN (钱航薇). Currently I am a Wallenberg - NTU Presidential Postdoctoral Fellow (PPF) in Lund University, Sweden working with Prof. Kalle Åström. Previously I spent my first year of PPF in Nanyang Technological University, Singapore with Prof. Chunyan Miao.

My research interests are unsupervised learning, transfer learning, kernel methods, and wearable-based studies.

I obtained my Ph.D. in School of Computer Science and Engineering at NTU, Singapore in 2020. I'm honored to work with Professor Sinno Jialin Pan. I received my B.E. from Automation, School of Information Science and Technology from Univeristy of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2015.
